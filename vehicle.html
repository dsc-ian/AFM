Overview

The historic Land Rover has been a mainstay of the Australian Army since 1958. Exemplifying the very best in practical hardware, the Perentie 110 was developed specifically for the Army, boasting increased payload and a galvanised chassis with rollover protection. Built from 1987 to 1998 the basic platform has been equipped for a range of roles with a range of equipment. These are tough-as-nails, go-anywhere, do-anything kit. And because they’re Land Rovers parts are easy to come by, making maintenance and service simple.
